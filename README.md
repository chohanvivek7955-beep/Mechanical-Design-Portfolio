# Mechanical Design Portfolio

Welcome to my Mechanical Design Portfolio!

This repository showcases a collection of CAD projects developed throughout my journey as a Mechanical Engineering student at **VIT Vellore**. The projects reflect my interests in robotics, product design, mechanical systems, and engineering problem-solving. Each project demonstrates my design process, technical skills, and focus on creating practical, manufacturable solutions.

---

## Featured Projects

### 🕷️ Hexapod Robot
A six-legged autonomous robot designed for traversing uneven terrain. This project focuses on modular mechanical design, structural optimization, inverse kinematics, and design for manufacturing.

### 🐕 Quadruped Robot
A four-legged robotic platform developed to study agile locomotion, lightweight structures, and efficient mechanical design for robotic applications.

### ⚙️ Planetary Gear System
A complete CAD model of a planetary gear assembly demonstrating compact power transmission, accurate gear meshing, and assembly design principles.
